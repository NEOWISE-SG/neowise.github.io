PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how NEOWISE ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our Artificial Intelligence ("AI") software ("Software"). This policy applies to all users of the Software and supplements our Terms and Conditions.

By using the Software and providing us with your information, you agree to be bound by this policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our software:

2.1 Personal Information

Information you provide voluntarily, which may include:

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Professional information (company name, job title)
  • Website and organizational information
  • Communication preferences

2.2 Usage Data

Information on how you access and use our software:

  • Browsing habits and preferences
  • Interactions with our software features
  • Feature usage patterns and frequency

2.3 Technical Data

Automatically collected information including:

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser type and version
  • Log data and error reports

We use your information for the following purposes:

4. INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

4.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Data analysis and analytics services
  • Customer support and communication tools
  • Payment processing services

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or if we believe that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect and defend our rights or property
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with our software
  • Protect the personal safety of users of the software or the public

4.3 Business Transfers

In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company, your information may be transferred to the successor entity.

4.4 Marketing Purposes

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

5. DATA SECURITY

5.1 Security Measures

We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Employee training on data protection

5.2 Security Limitations

Despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5.3 Your Responsibilities

We encourage you to take steps to keep your personal information safe, including choosing a strong password and keeping it confidential, and logging out of your account after each session.

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

6.1 Access and Control

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: The right to know what personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: The right to have inaccurate personal information corrected
  • Deletion: The right to have your personal information deleted in certain circumstances
  • Restriction: The right to limit how we use your personal information
  • Portability: The right to receive your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: The right to object to certain uses of your information

6.2 Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions included in these communications or by contacting us directly.

6.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your rights or if you have any questions about your personal information, please contact us using the details provided in the "Contact Information" section.

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

7.1 Types of Technologies We Use

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our software and hold certain information. These technologies include:

  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device
  • Web beacons: Electronic images that help us deliver cookies and count visits
  • Local storage: HTML5 storage mechanisms that store data on your device

7.2 Purposes of These Technologies

We use these technologies for various purposes, including:

  • Authentication and security
  • Preferences and functionality
  • Analytics and research
  • Advertising and marketing (with your consent)

7.3 Your Choices

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our software.

7.4 Online Tracking Opt-Out

When you visit or log in to our website, cookies and similar technologies may be used by our online data partners or vendors to associate these activities with other personal information. You may opt out of receiving targeted advertising by visiting https://app.retention.com/optout.
